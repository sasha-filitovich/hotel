Welcome to Pri Marusi

Pri Marusi guesthouse is located in Kranjska Gora, a popular tourist resort, 450 meters from the center. Kranjska Gora is located on the border of Slovenia's Triglav National Park. The park has a wide range of outdoor activities for guests: various bicycle routes, hiking trails of different difficulty levels, that pass through mountain peaks with passes, lakes, waterfalls and gorges. In Kranjska Gora you can find tennis courts, swimming pools and a golf course. In winter there is a large selection of ski slopes, ski schools are open for both children and adults. There is a large selection of sports equipment rental for summer and winter sports activities. The skating ice rink is open in winter.

Our guesthouse offers accommodation with a shared lounge, free private parking, a garden, free Wi-Fi, luggage storage and a dedicated room for drying sports equipment and storing skis and bicycles. Each room at the guesthouse is equipped with a desk. All rooms at Pri Marusi are equipped with a safe and smart TV with cable channels. Еvery morning Continental breakfast is served for our guests. There are more than 20 restaurants, cafes and bars in Kranjska Gora, where you can enjoy various dishes of Slovenian, Serbian and Italian cuisine.
